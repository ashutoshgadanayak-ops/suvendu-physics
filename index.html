Our Curriculums

Targeted training programs.

JEE / NEET Focus

Mechanics & Thermodynamics

Grade 11 Students & Repeaters

A rigorous foundation in classical Newtonian mechanics, fluid dynamics, and thermodynamics. Students learn to handle complex multi-force systems and rotational kinematics.

Key Modules

  • Rotational Dynamics & Torque
  • Work-Energy-Power Proofs
  • Laws of Thermodynamics
  • Gravitation & Orbit Mechanics
Advanced Electromagnetism

Electrodynamics, Optics & Modern Physics

Grade 12 Students & Repeaters

Focuses on field theory, electromagnetic induction, wave optics, and quantum physics. We translate abstract field concepts into logical, step-by-step vector calculations.

Key Modules

  • Coulomb's Law & Gauss Theorem
  • Electromagnetic Induction
  • Wave Optics & Interference
  • Photoelectric Effect & Bohr Model
Foundation Stream

Olympiad & Pre-Foundation Physics

Grade 9 & 10 Students

Introduces young students to higher-level concepts using intuitive models. We emphasize logical reasoning and primary experimentation over formula-plugging.

Key Modules

  • Mathematical Methods (Basic Calculus)
  • Kinematics & Graph Analysis
  • Ray Optics & Lenses
  • Current Electricity Foundations
