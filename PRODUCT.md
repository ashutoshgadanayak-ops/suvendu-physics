# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
High school and college students seeking elite physics training, along with their parents who seek structured, high-performing academic tutoring. They value clarity, results, academic rigor, and professional mentorship.

## Product Purpose
A premium landing page for SUVENDU PHYSICS, a top-tier physics coaching center led by Subhendu Dash. The page aims to present the institute's academic excellence, course offerings, and track record, and convert visitors into enrolled students or consultation leads.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Trustworthy, Cinematic, Elite. It leverages a "Midnight Lab" aesthetic—a dark projection-room feel where physics formulas and concepts stand out in high contrast.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered layouts with overlapping boxes (must be highly readable and simple on mobile viewports).
- Cheap-looking stock photos of generic classroom environments.
- Bright, neon-saturated educational landing pages that look spammy.

## Design Principles
- **Uncluttered Mobile Rhythm**: Ensure generous breathing room and simple vertical stacking on small viewports so students and parents can digest information easily.
- **Cinematic Framing**: Put physics content (mockups of lecture notes, results, testimonial quotes) in structured containers that "recede" into the dark background, highlighting the text and content.
- **Academic Restraint**: Let the size of typography and the single chromatic accent (vivid blue) define hierarchy rather than decorative flourishes.
- **Parent-Friendly Contrast**: While maintaining the dark aesthetic, ensure that contrast remains extremely high (Carbon Vellum text on Obsidian backgrounds) for older users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body copy.
- Clear typographic hierarchy for screen readers.
- Respect `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` for any fade-in transitions.
